HTML is made up of two tags:
<> - opening tag
</> - closing tag

HTML also has 3 categories/hierachies of tags:

parent tag - <html> </html>

sub-parent tags - <head> </head>, <body> </body>

child tags - <title> </title>, <style> </style>, <p> </p> <h1> </h1> .... <h6> </h6>



3 types of styling:

Inline styling
Internal Styling
External Styling
